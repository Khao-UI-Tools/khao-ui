# Fonts & Font Sizes

## (@der-reiskoch/khao-malet v{dependencies['@der-reiskoch/khao-malet']})

## Title Font (Montserrat)

Used for headings and titles with **font-weight: 700**

<Typeset
  fontFamily='"Montserrat", Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif, system-ui'
  fontSizes={[24, 28, 32, 40]}
  fontWeight={700}
  sampleText="The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog"
/>

**Typography tokens:** `--khao-sys-size-typography-6` (24px), `--khao-sys-size-typography-7` (28px), `--khao-sys-size-typography-8` (32px), `--khao-sys-size-typography-10` (40px)

## Body Font (Ubuntu)

Used for body text and paragraphs with **font-weight: 400**

<Typeset
  fontFamily='"Ubuntu", Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif, system-ui'
  fontSizes={[14, 16, 20]}
  fontWeight={400}
  sampleText="Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua."
/>

**Typography tokens:** `--khao-sys-size-typography-3` (14px), `--khao-sys-size-typography-4` (16px), `--khao-sys-size-typography-5` (20px)

## Complete Typography Scale

All available font sizes (using Ubuntu font family):

<Typeset
  fontFamily='"Ubuntu", Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif, system-ui'
  fontSizes={[12, 14, 16, 20, 24, 28, 32, 36, 40]}
  fontWeight={400}
  sampleText="Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet"
/>

**Available tokens:**
- `--khao-sys-size-typography-2`: 12px (base × 3)
- `--khao-sys-size-typography-3`: 14px (base × 3.5)
- `--khao-sys-size-typography-4`: 16px (base × 4)
- `--khao-sys-size-typography-5`: 20px (base × 5)
- `--khao-sys-size-typography-6`: 24px (base × 6)
- `--khao-sys-size-typography-7`: 28px (base × 7)
- `--khao-sys-size-typography-8`: 32px (base × 8)
- `--khao-sys-size-typography-9`: 36px (base × 9)
- `--khao-sys-size-typography-10`: 40px (base × 10)

_Base size: `--khao-ref-base-size-typography`: 4px_
